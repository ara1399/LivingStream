Interactive Living Stream

Fall 2024 – Spring 2025

Project Overview

The Living Stream Project creates an interactive aquatic life environment for educational and public spaces. Using a projector, depth sensor, and custom software, it simulates native fish species that react to user movements in real time.

Team & Sponsor

Sponsor: Jason Knight - United States Army Corps of Engineers (USACE)

Background

This project is designed to engage visitors by providing an interactive projection where the water splashes and fish move in response to human activity. It creates a dynamic and educational simulation of native aquatic life, ideal for teaching and interactive learning.
